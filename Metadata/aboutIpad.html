Help

Navigation

  • Drag with your finger to rotate, pinch to zoom.
  • Use two fingers to move around the worm.
  • Tap three times to reset the view.

Slider

  • Use the opacity slider on the left to reveal layers.
  • Click on the toggle at the bottom of the slider to control layers individually.

Selection

  • Use the search icon at the upper right to search, or tap on the cells to select them.
  • Click on the background to undo selection.

About

By purchasing this app you are directly supporting the OpenWorm project: thank you!

OpenWorm is an open source project dedicated to creating the world’s first virtual organism in a computer, starting with a C.elegans nematode. Find out more at OpenWorm.org.
